The Ultimate Upholstery Clean Way

The Ultimate Clean has a 7 step cleaning process which enables the best Upholstery Clean available in Perth. Every step is designed to get your upholstery sparkling clean and help it to stay clean for longer.

Pre-inspection and dye migration test.

We make sure the fabric is colour fast and assess any tears or deterioration.

Pre-vacuum

An essential step in Upholstery cleaning, we use a purpose designed hand piece on a powerful vacuum system to remove hairs and fine soil from the upholstery before cleaning.

Pre-treatment

With soft brushes or pads we loosen soiling from the fibre and agitate the pre-spray. This helps the final look of our carpet cleaning and helps to clean your carpet up to 50% better.

Hand Scrub.

A pre-treatment is not enough to completely break down the oils in the fabric. We hand scrub the fabric with a horse hair brush or microfiber glove designed specifically for cleaning your family’s Upholstery.

Extraction Rinse

Our powerful hot truck-mount upholstery cleaner is then used to flush out the dirt and cleaning agents from your upholstery achieving tempreatures around 130oC. Extra drying passes are performed for quick drying.

Fabric Protector

We can apply an Anti-Soiling protector on the furniture after cleaning. This helps to repel the oils on the skin and helps the upholstery stay cleaner for longer!

High speed air mover

Our air mover is placed into position to help with the drying process after cleaning. We use the best equipment in Perth to give your treasured upholstery a deep down clean and fresh appearance. Contact us today to bring your furniture back to life.

An amazing finishing touch!

Protective Furniture Tabs

Plastic or foam block tabs placed under furniture lift it off your carpet preventing staining or possible damage during drying.

Rapid Drying Systems

The combination of our high speed air movers, drying bonnets, grooming tools, ‘green’ glides on our wands and powerful truck mounted machines all increase air flow over your carpet fibres
We understand fast-drying carpet is important so during the truck mounted carpet cleaning process we even have dedicated drying strokes to remove excess water.
